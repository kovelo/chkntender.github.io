3. What Information Is Collected and Why

Data Collected:

The data is collected in order to offer in-app personalization of relevant content, analytical purposes, and to offer first-party advertisements. The data is never linked to a user's identity.

Retention:
Collected data is stored indefinitely to help improve the service and app.

3.1 AdMob

AdMob is Google's mobile app advertising platform designed specifically for app developers. In order to understand Google's use of Data, consult Google's partner policy. AdMob is used for generic third-party advertisements inside of the app. None of the usage data collected for first-party purposes above are shared with Google or their partners.

Personal Data:

  • Online identifiers, including cookie identifiers
  • IP addresses

  • Client identifiers

Retention:
Google anonymizes the log data by removing part of the IP address (after 9 months) and cookie information (after 18 months).
