How It Works

From indecision to dinner in three easy steps.

1

Find Nearby Spots

Open Chicken Tender and the app automatically loads restaurants near your location. It uses Bluetooth to silently discover everyone nearby who has the app open — no sharing, no setup.

2

Swipe to Vote

Each restaurant appears as a card. Swipe right if you’re interested, left to pass. Everyone plays on their own device at the same time — it only takes a few minutes, and it’s genuinely fun.

3

See Your Matches

When everyone’s done swiping, Chicken Tender reveals the restaurants you all liked. Pick one and go — the debate is officially over.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Chicken Tender?
Chicken Tender is a free mobile app that makes it easy for groups to decide where to eat. Everyone swipes on nearby restaurants, and the app shows you the places the whole group liked — no more endless back-and-forth.
Is Chicken Tender free?
Free to download. The app is supported by ads so the core experience costs nothing. If you’d rather go ad-free, there’s a single one-time in-app purchase to remove ads permanently — no subscription required.
How does the group matching work?
Everyone just opens the app. Chicken Tender uses Bluetooth Low Energy to automatically discover all nearby players who have the app open — no invites, no links to share, zero setup. Everyone swipes on the same set of nearby restaurants, and when the group is done the app reveals the spots everyone liked.
What devices are supported?
Chicken Tender works on iPhone, iPad, and iPod Touch and on Android smartphones and tablets.
Does it work for just two people, or even solo?
Absolutely. Chicken Tender works great for couples on date night, families picking dinner, and large groups of friends. It also works perfectly on your own — if you’re traveling or just exploring a new neighborhood, use it solo to swipe through nearby restaurants and find somewhere great to eat.
